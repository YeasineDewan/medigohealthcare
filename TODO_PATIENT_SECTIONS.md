## Features Implemented:

### Patient Registration:
- Multi-step form with progress indicator
- Personal info, contact details, medical info, insurance
- Auto-generate patient ID
- Photo upload capability

### Patient Records:
- Advanced search and filters
- Table and Grid view modes
- Patient statistics dashboard
- Quick view details modal

### Medical History:
- Timeline view with medical events
- Diagnoses, treatments, lab results
- Allergies and conditions tracking
- Family history and immunizations

### Appointments:
- All appointments view with status
- Filter by status, doctor, department
- Quick actions (confirm, cancel, complete)
- Detailed appointment modal

### Billing:
- Invoice management with status tracking
- Payment history and insurance claims
- Financial statistics dashboard
- Export capabilities
